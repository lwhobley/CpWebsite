What is ComplianceDaddy?

We built the compliance platform operators actually deserve.

750,000+ licensed hospitality venues in the US navigate an average of 23 distinct compliance domains. Most rely on binders, spreadsheets, and institutional memory. A single missed permit can cost everything.

ComplianceDaddy replaces paper-based compliance workflows with intelligent digital audits, AI-driven checklists, and automated reporting—purpose-built for bars, restaurants, nightclubs, and hotels.

From liquor board compliance to health code audits, fire inspections to employment law—one platform covers it all. Pre-launch. Join the waitlist for founding access.
